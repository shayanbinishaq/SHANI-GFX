Payment Terms & Policies

Project Deposits

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to begin work on any project. This deposit secures your place in our schedule and allows us to allocate resources to your project.

Final Payment

The remaining balance is due upon project completion and before the delivery of final files. Once final payment is received, all agreed-upon files will be delivered.

Refund Policy

Initial deposits are non-refundable as they compensate for time already invested in your project. For projects canceled after work has begun, payment will be prorated based on work completed.

Late Payments

Payments not received within 14 days of the invoice date may incur a 5% late fee. Projects with outstanding balances may be put on hold until payment is received.
