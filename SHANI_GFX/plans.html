Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in each plan?

Each plan includes different levels of design services, from basic logo and business card design in our Basic plan to comprehensive brand identity and marketing materials in our Premium plan. You can see the detailed breakdown of features in each plan above.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. We'll prorate the difference and apply it to your new plan. Contact our support team to assist you with the upgrade process.

How do revisions work?

Revisions allow you to request changes to the designs we create. The Basic plan includes 3 revision rounds, while Pro and Premium plans offer unlimited revisions to ensure your complete satisfaction.

What if I need services not listed in the plans?

We offer custom solutions for specific requirements. Contact us to discuss your needs, and we'll create a tailored package for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency. Visit our Payment Methods page for more details.
